Monitor your daily digital consumption and set healthy limits for apps and websites that tend to steal your attention.

Usage Analytics

How It Works

Four simple steps to better digital habits.

1

Connect with Peers

Add friends and form a peer accountability group to help each other develop better digital habits. You can invite existing contacts or find like-minded accountability buddies within the app.

2

Set Usage Goals

Establish daily screen time limits and goals that work for you. The app will help you track progress and share updates with your peer group when you exceed your limits.

3

Receive Peer Support

When you reach your time threshold, your peers automatically get notified. They can send encouragement or check in on you to help keep you accountable.

4

Review & Improve

Check your stats and peer feedback to continuously improve your digital habits. Compare your trends over time and celebrate your progress with your accountability group.
